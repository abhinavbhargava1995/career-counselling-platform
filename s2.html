L.I.C. Agent

LIC agents are professionals who work in the Life Insurance Corporation of India. They are actually intermediaries who create ties between LIC clients and LIC. Those who satisfy the eligibility requirements set up by the LIC can become LIC Agents. These professionals, in their daily affairs deal with clients, explaining them the different types of insurance policies, like medi-claims, recurring savings schemes and so on which are offered by LIC. They also convince the clients and make them take a policy. In addition, they have to collect the insurance policy amount from the clients and pay it to Life Insurance Corporation on a monthly or yearly basis.
